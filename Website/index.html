Better for Future

College is the time that people start to be independent and prepare for the future ahead. Although, if you start off wrong, then your future will be ruin. Therefore, you need start planning out for your future when you step into college. Beside that, the years through out college is not easy either. Working and going to school at the same time, or heavy class-work-load could lead to stress and depression. With T & M for Now and Future, we help you manage your time and save money for your future. In addition, the app is not only available for college students, it is for everybody that need help managing their time and money.

Be Flexible On Your Time

What are the problems?

  1. Working and going to school at the same time
  2. School activities
  3. Friends and parties

How to solve it?

  1. Keep up with your work at school, don't procastinate
  2. Make schedule of work and school the same everyweek
  3. Make sure to complete the important task such as homeworks and work before doing any other activities

Listing solutions and the problem out could get students to be aware of it. Although, how could students make it work for them. Now, with T & M for Now and Future, students don't have to worry about it much because this app would help manage the time for students. It clearly set up the schedule and showing the task everyday just by entering the information such as homework for which classes, when is it due, working schedule, or classes schedule.

Stay Alert, Don't Be Broke

Why saving money?

  • Emergencies problems
  • Pay for loans after graduate

Saving is necessary for students and any body in this world. People should always have back up money to prepare for the worst because this life is not a straight line. With the T & M for Now and Future app, people could save money in a better way. The app will show user how much money they have been saving. It also remind user to put in saving money for each month.
